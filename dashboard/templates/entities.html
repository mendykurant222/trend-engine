❓ How to read this page
  • Mentions 14d — total attention volume over the last 14 days, summed across all sources (posts, searches, videos, articles, filings). It answers "how much noise?", not "which way?".
  • Δ week — the direction you asked about: this week's volume vs the week before. ▲ rising (+25% or more), ▼ falling (−25% or less), ≈ flat, 🆕 new (first seen within 7 days — no prior week to compare).
  • Sources — how many independent platforms mention it. 1 source = chatter; 3+ sources = something real is moving (hover to see which).
  • Sparkline — the 30-day shape. 🟠 marks the peak day (hover for the value); the last dot is today. A right-rising line + multiple sources = a trend candidate before it becomes an official trend.
  • Phase — where the story is: 🌱 climbing (peak is today/yesterday — still building), steady, 🏔 past peak (the peak was a week+ ago — the story is already told). The 🌱 Early radar button combines this with "small + multi-source" to show pre-peak candidates only.
